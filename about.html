INDIETALKS

IndieTalks was made in year 2023 where in the main purpose site is to promote these indie bands and artist. The site is free promotional to help our hardworking artists, these Indie bands needs promoting, crediting, and recognizing to get a proper publicity. Nowadays. it's hard to recognize them cause some people didn't stream their works because they lack of proper exposure and moderators focused only on pop music.

What's best thing about indie artist is they give their best to produce a really unique quality of lyrics and music. Indie aspriring story gives different vibes because most of their artworks gives chills that you will feel the empathy towards them and you can feel what are they trying to express. Some of the lyrics gives dark stories, scenario in our community, and lastly it shout genuine feelings.

INDIETALKS Developer

IndieTalks developer are musically inclined and loves indie bands that's why the site was developed. First thing that comes to developer mind is how can I help these artists and indiebands to promote themselves? The idea of giving a solution to this problem is essential to them to have an ease to promote themselves in the most easiest way. Social media platforms promotes only pop music and content moderators modify their content to get more engagements from viewers, getting to know this people or so called "Indie artist" is very heart warming because it gives some very unique insights and thoughts.

Developer uses bootstrap version 5.2.0 as references of templates to create a site with the help font awesome and Roboto fonts. The color pallete emphasis the very indie design with the combination of black and white shades.
